This privacy summary provides a concise overview of how Neuro AI Study Planner collects, uses, and protects your personal information. For complete details, see our [Privacy Policy](privacy.html).

**Educational Data**:

2. **Improvement**

## Data Sharing

### Who We Share With

**OpenAI**:
- Study plan requests (anonymized)
- No personal identifying information
- Subject to OpenAI's privacy policy

**Firebase (Google)**:
- Authentication data
- App analytics
- Crash reports
- Performance data

**Third-Party Services**:
- Payment processors
- Email services
- Analytics providers

### What We DON'T Share

- Personal identifying information
- Account passwords
- Private study plans (without permission)
- Payment details
- Private messages

## Data Security

### Protection Measures

**Encryption**:
- All data transmitted via HTTPS

- Database encryption at rest
- Secure token handling

**Access Controls**:
- Restricted employee access
- Role-based permissions
- Audit logging
- Regular security reviews

**Infrastructure**:
- Firebase security standards
- Regular backups
- Monitoring and alerts
- Incident response procedures
